Cortado takes your privacy very seriously. While your sensitive data is stored on your device, both within the Cortado app and in Apple HealthKit, Cortado does not upload your sensitive data to the Internet. Cortado does track your app usage using third-party analytics providers. This is standard practice among nearly every app on the App Store. We do our best to minimize the amount of data we are gathering, and this will never include your location. Additionally, analyics data gathered is anonymized.
