WhatsApplebee's

http://whatsapplebees.com

Role: UX Architecture, Visual Design and Iconography, Code

WhatsApplebee's is a real-time anonymous chat app that can only be used if you're currently inside an Applebee's restaurant.

Specifically, if you are inside an Applebee's in the U.S., you have access to a single chat room connecting you with all other users at all other Applebee's.

So many of my talented friends and peers spend their time raising millions of dollars in VC funding to build silly, meaningless apps. I wanted to create something so outlandish that should be obvious at a glance that it's a joke, but is rendered believable by the absurdity of the current tech ecosystem.

More importantly, it actually exists. There are plenty of fake product landing pages lampooning startup culture; I wanted to take it the step further and actually turn it into a real, functioning app.

Results

Besides receiving a great deal of press coverage in places such as CNN and The Verge, user engagement has been high. It has been downloaded thousands of times, has a 4.5-star rating on the App Store, and has new messages posted every day (meaning people are actively using it from within an Applebee's).

I'm proudest of how WhatsApplebee's continues to delight new audiences. I get an email or a tweet maybe once a day where someone read about the app months ago, forgot about it, and then happened to find themselves in an Applebee's. The moment they remember it, open it up, and find that it actually works is priceless.
