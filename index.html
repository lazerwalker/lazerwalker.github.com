Hi. I'm Mike.

I make experimental games, interactive art, and software tools. Above all, I like to make things that color the way people view the world in interesting ways.

These days, I work with the Playful Systems group at the MIT Media Lab, where my research mostly focuses on embodied narrative and storytelling in public spaces. Previously, I've worked on great products and teams such as Words With Friends, Timehop, and Pivotal Labs.

I firmly believe the best way to get anything done is through intense collaboration with a small group of passionate and creative people. I love meeting new people to jam with, so feel free to get in touch for any reason.
