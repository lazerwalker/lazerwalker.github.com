Tenuki Privacy Policy

This app uploads crash and performance data to Sentry.io, using an anonymous identifier. For more information, see Sentry's privacy policy at https://docs.sentry.io/security-legal-pii/security/mobile-privacy/. This app does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies, and does not otherwise collect personal data.

If you choose to log in with your online-go.com or Pandanet/IGS accounts, this app will send your login credentials to online-go.com or igs.joyjoy.net (respectively) for authentication. No OGS login credentials are stored by this app; we do store your Pandanet credentials in secure storage, using standard iOS encryption. Information such as move data and chat messages will also be transmitted to either service in order to display your games. You can refer to their privacy policies at https://online-go.com/docs/privacy-policy and https://pandanet-igs.com/communities/pandanet/411.
