Tenuki Privacy Policy

This app does not collect any personal data. It does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies. If you choose to log in with your online-go.com account, this app will send your login credentials to online-go.com for authentication. No login credentials are stored by this app. Move data will also be transmitted to online-go.com in order to display your games, in which case you should refer to their privacy policy at https://online-go.com/docs/privacy-policy.
